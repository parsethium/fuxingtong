About Us

Qingdao Fuxingtong Forging Co., Ltd., founded in 2015 and headquartered in Qingdao, Shandong Province, China, is a leading precision component manufacturer for the Oil & Gas and Aerospace industries. As a privately held company, we are committed to delivering high-quality components with our motto: "Good is not enough, Be great". Our expertise and innovation make us a trusted supplier for critical engineering applications worldwide.

"Our strength lies in uniting advanced manufacturing with a deep understanding of industrial needs. We don't just supply materials — we enable progress in the world's most demanding sectors: aerospace, energy, and engineering."

Mike Fang, CEO

Manufacturing Capabilities

We provide end-to-end manufacturing solutions for high-precision components, covering every stage from raw materials to final assembly:

  • Forging: Closed-die forging, Isothermal forging, Precision forging for aerospace and oil & gas applications
  • Casting: Precision casting for complex metal components
  • Sheet Metal Forming: High-accuracy forming solutions
  • CNC Machining: 3, 4, and 5-axis CNC machining for precision components
  • Assembly: Final module integration for reliable performance

Special Processes

Our special processes ensure components meet the most demanding standards:

  • Welding: for structural integrity
  • Brazing: for precision metal joining

Quality Inspection

Quality control is our top priority. Fuxingtong is equipped with advanced laboratory facilities for both Destructive Testing (DT) and Non-Destructive Testing (NDT):

  • Chemical and mechanical analysis
  • Fluorescent Penetrant Inspection (FPI)
  • Radiographic Testing (RT)
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT) and Immersion UT
  • Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) inspection
  • 3D scanning for dimensional accuracy

Collaboration

We value strong partnerships with clients and industry leaders. By understanding project requirements and maintaining transparent communication, we deliver tailored solutions that enhance performance, efficiency, and reliability.

Certificates

Fuxingtong adheres to international quality standards, holding ISO and industry-specific certifications. Our certifications demonstrate our commitment to superior manufacturing processes, material quality, and precise component delivery.
