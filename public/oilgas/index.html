Oil & Gas

Land-Base Gas Turbine & Energy

Components Sub-header: Heavy-duty manufacturing solutions for high-temperature, high-pressure energy systems.

Explore Capabilities

Industrial Gas Turbine (IGT) Components

IGT Blades & Vanes

Large-scale precision forgings for land-based gas turbines, designed to withstand sustained high-cycle operation.

Hot-Section Parts

Combustion chamber segments, transition pieces, and hot-gas-path components built for extreme thermal loads.

Nozzles & Stators

Intricately designed stator assemblies and turbine nozzles with optimized airflow paths to conform to standards.

Power Generation Hardware

  • Fuel and Air Distribution Systems: High-flow fuel delivery systems for efficient gas and liquid fuel combustion.
  • Ignition & Control Components: Spark plugs, igniters, and temperature sensors that ensure safe start-up and condition monitoring.
  • Combustion Hardware: Swirled combustors, liners and oxidation panels for clean, low-emission systems.

Specialized Energy Applications

Wear-Resistant Parts

Hard-coated shafts, components designed for abrasive and corrosive environments typical in oil extraction.

Pressure-Containing Components

Thick-walled pipes, flanges, and housings engineered for sub-sea and wellhead high-pressure scenarios.

Fuel Delivery Systems

Heavy-duty fuel nozzles and pumps adapted for on-site extraction-support packages.

Materials & Durability

  • High-Temperature Superalloys: Nickel and Cobalt-based alloys specifically curated for creep-resistance and fatigue endurance in energy applications.
  • Corrosion-Resistant Coatings: Thermal sprays, hot and diffusion coatings to defend parts against continuous heat and humid environments.

Modules & Assemblies

  • Turbine & Compressor Modules: Fully assembled sub-systems integrated via electron beam welding and diffusion bonding.
  • Combustion Modules: Complete combustion assemblies ready for turbine integration.
