Inspection

Our Inspection and Quality Assurance operations deliver unmatched quality assurance solutions to ensure that all components meet the most stringent aerospace and industrial standards. We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and certified procedures to maintain zero-defect integrity, product reliability, and performance of critical components.

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

We offer a complete set of NDT methods for subsurface flaw/crack inspection and internal components, including:

  • Radiographic Testing (RT/X-ray): Internal defect mapping
  • Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT): Surface-crack inspection and defects
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT): Internal defect/bulk verification (Discontinuities, thickness/grain, impactions, voids)
  • Magnetic Particle Testing (MT): Ferromagnetic-material surface-crack detection
  • Eddy-Current Testing (ECT): Conductive tube / surface-flaw detection / conductivity/material thickness identification (UT-compatible)
  • Visual Inspection: Borescope, microscopy, and direct optical examination

Material Testing

  • Chemical Analysis: spectroscopy, cross-section reviews, X-ray
  • Microstructure Testing: metallography, grain-size analysis
  • Mechanical Testing: tensile, hardness, fatigue, creep, impact, and property evaluations

Dimensional & Functional Inspection

We perform complete dimensional and functional inspections for all parts, machines, and surfaces, including:

  • CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machines) - high-precision and fixtureless inspection procedures
  • Laser Scanning & 3D Scanning: Point-cloud comparison for complex geometries and reverse engineering verification
  • Optical Inspection: Vision systems, microscopes, comparators
  • Go/No-Go Gauges and Custom Fixtures - rapid indicators for serial production

Applications

  • Casting components
  • Forged and heat treated components
  • Formed parts (tubes, shells, and sheet-stamped parts)
  • Machined components
  • Repaired/refurbished parts, assemblies, and delivery lot returns

Key Benefits

Industry-certified laboratories for stringent aero-engineering standards including AS9100 and customer-specific requirements, using AS/ASTM and AMS standards.

  • Ensures integrity and verified mechanical properties
  • Accelerates decision-making with certified structural integrity
  • Validation and stringent aerospace and industrial standards
  • Efficient root-cause analysis and corrective-action procedures
  • Advanced non-destructive and dimensional evaluation parameters
