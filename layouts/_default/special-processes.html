Special Process & Assembly

Our Special Processes division encompasses advanced surface treatments, joining techniques, and final-assembly operations, ensuring the reliability of high-performance components. Utilizing state-of-the-art equipment, precise process control, and certified procedures, we deliver components that meet the most demanding aerospace and industrial standards.

Advanced Special Processes

We offer a comprehensive range of specialized processes to: Optimize component properties, longevity, and surface performance, including:

  • Heat Treatment Services: aging, stress-relief, and customized thermal cycles for superalloys and specialty steels.
  • Surface Treatments & Coatings:
  • Thermal spray coatings (plasma, HVOF)
  • Hard coatings and wear-resistant layers
  • Corrosion-resistant coatings for marine and land-based environments
  • Shot Peening & Surface Finishing: improving fatigue life and surface integrity
  • Precision grinding, lapping, and honing
  • Electroplating / Anodizing / Passivation
  • Welding & Brazing: for repair, assembly, and production
  • TIG, MIG, laser welding, and specialized repair techniques
  • Design optimization for length reduction and material efficiency

Applications

  • Turbine modules
  • Combustion chamber modules
  • Engine housings
  • Repair and refurbish structural components and engine hardware

Assembly Capabilities

Our assembly operations enable the final integration of complex components into fully-functional assemblies, using advanced and precise assembly techniques:

  • Fitting & Mechanical Assembly
  • Precision assembly of shafts, vanes, and wheel modules
  • Welding
  • Stator assembly & Vane packs
  • Laser welding for structural and fine-welded components
  • Component Assembly & Balancing
  • Rotor balancing (low-speed, high-speed)
  • Assembly for unique assemblies
  • Sealing & Testing of Pressurized Parts
  • Leak testing and pressure verification
  • QA/QC

Key Benefits

  • Enhanced life of products.
  • Superior properties.
  • Reduced lead time.
  • In-house capabilities that streamline production-ready for services in seamless environments.
