Advanced Powders

Specialized powders engineered for surface enhancement and next-generation manufacturing.

Metal Powders (Additive Manufacturing)

Designed for 3D printing (SLM/DMLS) and thermal spray applications with controlled particle size and morphology for superior flowability.

  • Alloys: Nickel superalloys (IN718, IN625), Titanium (Ti-64), Cobalt-Chrome, Stainless Steels (316L, 17-4PH), and Maraging steels.
  • Applications: Rapid prototyping, complex geometry production, and component repair.

Coating Powders (Surface Protection)

Metallic and ceramic powders for Thermal Barrier Coatings (TBC), wear resistance, and corrosion prevention. Compatible with APS and HVOF processes.

  • Composition: MCrAlY systems (NiCrAlY, CoCrAlY), Carbide blends (WC-Co), and Ceramics (YSZ, Alumina).
  • Benefits: Optimal adhesion and density for extreme environment protection.
