Machining

Our Machining Division provides high-precision processing for complex aerospace and industrial components, ensuring each part satisfies strict dimensional, surface-quality, and functional requirements. We integrate advanced equipment, skilled technicians, and rigorous inspection routines, upholding repeatability and traceability across a wide range of materials and geometries.

Advanced Machining Infrastructure

Using our comprehensive suite of high-performance equipment, we can machine complex equiaxed, directionally solidified (DS), and single-crystal (SX) castings, along with forged, formed, and other specialized materials and components, which is supported by:

  • Edge-precision grinding, machines for tight-tolerance surfaces
  • Row-drilling (multi-spindle) units for casting EDM with well-to-power operations
  • Precision CNC turning and milling centers
  • Specialized fixtures and automated clamping systems
  • Process-controlled finishing, Hon, and ultrasonic cleaning

Wide Range of Materials

  • Nickel-based superalloys for high-temperature component
  • Cobalt-based alloys for wear-resistant parts
  • High-strength stainless steels for structural and hot-section applications
  • Heat-resistant specialty alloys for power-generation equipment
  • Titanium and aluminum alloys for structural/engine components

High-Precision Features & Operations

Our machining operations adhere to strict tolerances and surface-finish requirements across all steps:

  • Post and platform machining for rotating and stationary components
  • Profile milling, contour shaping, and drilling
  • Hole-finishing (reaming, honing) with strict positional accuracy
  • Cooling-hole drilling (deep holes, EDM, or ECM technologies)
  • Tip grinding, edge finishing, and precision root shaping
  • In-process SPC tracking, dimensional verification, and real-time geometries
  • Machining of thin-wall structures and complex internal cavities

Products & Applications

  • Finished turbine blades and vanes (all platforms and airfoil and structural components
  • Hot-section and structural components
  • Engine hardware and housings
  • Aerospace engine components
  • Industrial gas-turbine finishing
