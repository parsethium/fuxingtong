Forming

Our Forming Division provides advanced solutions for shaping high-performance components used in aerospace engines, industrial gas turbines, and other demanding applications. Utilizing state-of-the-art forming technologies and precise process control, we produce components with complex geometries, excellent dimensional accuracy, and superior mechanical properties.

Advanced Forming Infrastructure

Our forming operations allow production of components with intricate shapes, thin walls, and tight tolerances, meeting the demanding requirements of aerospace and industrial applications.

  • Hydro-forming and super plastic forming systems for complex thin-walled components
  • Cold and hot sheet forming presses for structural and engine parts
  • Precision tube bending and expansion equipment for fuel nozzles, cooling tubes, and ducts
  • Automated material handling and pre-heating systems
  • Process-controlled annealing and stress-relief lines

Wide Range of Materials

  • Nickel-based super alloys for hot-section components.
  • Cobalt-based alloys for wear-resistant parts.
  • High-temperature stainless steels.
  • Titanium alloys for aerospace and industrial components.
  • Heat-resistant specialty alloys for energy systems.

Products & Applications

  • Combustion chambers and liners
  • Fuel nozzles and injectors
  • Thin-walled vanes and ducting components
  • Cooling tubes and structural engine parts
  • Aerospace and industrial gas-turbine components
Inspection Capabilities

Key Fabrication Steps

  • Material Preparation: Preheat and condition sheets, tubes, or blanks according to alloy specifications.
  • Hydroforming / Superplastic Forming: Shape thin-walled and complex components, such as combustion chambers and fuel nozzles.
  • Sheet & Tube Forming: Cold or hot forming of structural parts, ducts, and vanes.
  • Precision Bending & Expansion: Produce precise tubing, cooling channels, and nozzle geometries.
  • Stress-Relief & Annealing: Enhance ductility, relieve forming stresses, and stabilize microstructure.
